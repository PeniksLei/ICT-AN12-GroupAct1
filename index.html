Mission

To produce competent and socially responsible professionals through innovative instructions, high-impact research, and sustainable extension services responsive to the diverse needs of glocal communities.

Makalikha ng mga bihasa at responsableng mga propesyonal sa pamamagitan ng makabago at mataas na kalidad ng pagtuturo, makabuluhang pananaliksik, at pangmatagalang serbisyong pang-ekstensyon na tumutugon sa iba’t ibang pangangailangan ng glokal na komunidad.

Vision

A smart and transnational university.

Isang smart at transnasyonal na unibersidad.

Philosophy

The Rizal Technological University believes in nurturing the creative potentials of Filipinos to excel in a dynamic world order and advocates commitment to global peace and sustainable development along with sense of moral responsibility and cultural patronage.

Naniniwala ang Rizal Technological University sa pangangalaga sa malikhaing potensyal ng mga Filipino upang manguna sa pabago-bagong takbo ng mundo at magtaguyod ng komitment patungo sa pandaigdigang kapayapaan at napananatiling pag-unlad kasabay ng moral na responsibilidad at kultural na pagtangkilik.

Departments

College of Engineering and Architecture (CEA): Includes departments such as Architecture, Civil Engineering, and various engineering specializations like Instrumentation and Control, Industrial, and Computer Engineering.

College of Business, Entrepreneurship, and Accountancy (CBEA): Offers programs in Accountancy, Entrepreneurship, and Office Administration.

College of Education (CEd): Offers Bachelor of Secondary Education majors in English, Math, Science, and Social Studies, and Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTED) program with several specialized majors, including Visual Graphic Design, Animation, Computer Systems Servicing, Garments Fashion and Design, Electronics Technology, and Welding and Fabrication Technology.

College of Arts and Sciences (CAS): Includes departments like Psychology and Mathematics.

Graduate School: Offers advanced degrees in Education, Public Administration, Business Administration, and Engineering.

Junior High School (RTU-JHS): Features specialized K to 12 curriculum with technology-focused subjects.
